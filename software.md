Here's a list of software that I've been involved with creating, either directly (code I wrote) or indirectly (devised methods or supervised). 

- [sylph](https://github.com/bluenote-1577/sylph) - ultrafast genome ANI queries and taxonomic profiling for shotgun metagenomic samples by abundance-corrected k-mer sketching.
- [floria](https://github.com/bluenote-1577/floria) - strain-level single-sample metagenomic read phasing and clustering by flow-based MEC haplotyping.
- [skani](https://github.com/bluenote-1577/skani) - fast and robust metagenomic average nucleotide identity (ANI) calculation by sparse k-mer chaining. 
- [mora](https://github.com/AfZheng126/MORA) - strain-level read assignment using abundance information (written by [Andrew Zheng](https://github.com/AfZheng126)). 
- [flopp](https://github.com/bluenote-1577/flopp) - fast polyploid haplotype phasing for long-read data. 
- [c2-CW](https://github.com/bluenote-1577/c2invariant_cw_code) - calculation of c2 invariant for graphs appearing in phi4 theory.
- [OPERA-MS](https://github.com/CSB5/OPERA-MS) - hybrid metagenomic assembler for long and short-reads.
