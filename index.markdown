Hi there! I'm Jim, a PhD student in the math department at the University of Toronto. I work in Prof. [Yun William Yu's](https://yunwilliamyu.net/content/) lab.

My research interests primarily lie in the applications of novel mathematical techniques to computational genomics. I like looking at computational problems involving sequencing data through a mathematical lens. 

I did my bachelor's degree at the University of British Columbia in Vancouver, where I studied engineering physics and mathematics.
